Some Lesser Known Facts About Diljit Dosanjh

  • Does Diljit Dosanjh smoke?: No
  • Does Diljit Dosanjh drink alcohol?: No
  • Diljit hails from a humble family with a modest income; as his father’s salary was around ₹5,000.

    Diljit Dosanjh’s Childhood Photo

  • From a young age, he got an inclination towards music and started performing at local gurudwaras.

    Diljit Dosanjh In Younger Days

  • He gave his first stage performance at a village, for the purpose of engaging the audience; as Master Saleem was late for his performance.
  • In 2000, he made his singing debut with the album ‘Ishq Da Uda Ada.’

  • He was originally named “Daljit” that he later changed to “Diljit” on the recommendation of his record producer, Rajinder Singh.
  • His song “Lak 28 Kudi Da” was a mega-hit in which Yo Yo Honey Singh was also featured.

  • His performance in the film ‘Jatt and Juliet’ became a turning point in his acting career and the film’s sequel was even a bigger hit.

  • In 2013, his singing breakthrough came with the song “Proper Patola” ft. Badshah. Moreover, it became the first Punjabi song to be featured on Vevo.

  • Apart from singing and acting, he is also a philanthropist; as he founded a foundation named Saanjh Foundation on his 30th birthday in 2013, which helps underprivileged children and senior citizens.

    Diljit Dosanjh’s Saanjh Foundation

  • He is one of the most in-demand stars in the ad world and has been a brand endorser for many brands as he has endorsed many brands like Coca-Cola and Flipkart Connect for Punjab.

    Diljit Dosanjh Endorsing Various Brands

  • He has a good sense of humour and holds a witty personality.
  • In 2017, there was a rumour in the social media that he had purchased a Cessna 340A Jet Plane. Later on, however, he clarified that he doesn’t own any jet; as it was just used to take him to a place in Rajasthan for a film shoot.

    Diljit Dosanjh Coming Out Of A Jet

  • His Bollywood debut ‘Udta Punjab’ (2016) was a big hit and made him earn his first Filmfare award.
  • Diljit is not very much comfortable with English and mostly prefers Punjabi over it. He also shared that he needs someone by his side when he interacts with someone who speaks English.
  • He personally dislikes explicit lyrics in songs.
  • He is a huge fan of American TV personality Kylie Jenner and is often seen commenting on her posts on various social media handles.

    Diljit Dosanjh Comments On Kylie Jenner’s Social Media Account

  • In 2018, he portrayed the role of the famous International hockey player- Sandeep Singh in his biopic ‘Soorma.’

  • Diljit initially refused to do Soorma; as he was never interested in doing a sports film, but Sandeep Singh’s inspiring story prompted him to take the role. Moreover, Sandeep Singh taught him how to play hockey.
  • He had rejected films of his most favourite directors from Bollywood; as he was asked to act without the turban.
  • Johnny Depp‘s role of “Captain Jack Sparrow” in ‘Pirates of the Caribbean’ has been his dream role.
